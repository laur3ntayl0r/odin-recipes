Chicken Pot Pie

A filling and simple chicken pot pie recipe, perfect for weeknights!

Ingredients
  • 1c cooked chicken, cubed
  • 9oz frozen mixed veggies, thawed
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1/2c milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1c Bisquick
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In 9" pie plate, mix chicken, veggies, and soup.
  3. Mix milk, egg, and Bisquick, then pour over chicken mixture.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
