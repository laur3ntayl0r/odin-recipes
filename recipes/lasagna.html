Lasagna

A delicious and straightforward lasagna made with turkey sausage and spinach.

Ingredients
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 4c cheese (mozzarella or italian blend)
  • 2 jars spicy spaghetti sauce
  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 1lb spicy turkey sausage
  • 16-32oz frozen spinach
Directions
  1. Brown ground turkey, then drain.
  2. Brown spicy turkey sausage, then drain.
  3. Cook 8-10 pieces of lasagna.
  4. Combine meat and sauce.
  5. Layer 9x13 pan from bottom to top, like so:
    • sauce
    • noodles
    • sauce
    • cheese
    • spinach
    • noodles
    • sauce
    • cheese
    • spinach
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
  7. Uncover and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes and then serve!
