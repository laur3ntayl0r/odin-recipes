Tuna Tempter

A yummy tuna casserole that's quick and easy!

Ingredients
  • 1 small can tuna, drained and flaked
  • 1 can cream of celery
  • 1/2c celery, chopped
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 8oz egg noodles
  • 1 soup can of milk
  • 1/4c buttered bread crumbs
  • 1c frozen peas (approx., optional)
Directions
  1. Cook noodles and drain.
  2. In a skillet, saute onions and celery in butter.
  3. Add cream of celery, milk (use cream of celery can to measure), and tuna to onions and celery.
  4. Add the above to a casserole dish along with noodles (and peas if desired), mix.
  5. Top with bread crumbs.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes.
